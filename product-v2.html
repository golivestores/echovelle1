What is the Akoya Strand? +

A timeless strand of 7.0–7.5mm AAA+ grade Akoya pearls, hand-selected from Japan's Mie Prefecture and hand-knotted on natural silk thread. Finished with a solid 18K white gold clasp — never plated, never hollow.

This is the necklace that anchors a jewelry collection: the piece worn at every milestone, passed between generations.

What's In The Box +

Every Echovelle piece arrives ready to gift — or to treasure.

  • Certificate of Authenticity — signed and numbered; guarantees grade, origin, and our lifetime promise.
  • Velvet-Lined Gift Box — soft interior, magnetic closure, FSC-certified outer shell.
  • Care Card — simple rituals for luster that lasts, straight from our master stringer.
  • Handwritten Note — add a message at checkout; we'll write it in ink, on cotton paper, by hand.
Jewelry Details +
  • Pearl Type — Akoya (saltwater), cultured in Mie Prefecture, Japan
  • Pearl Size — 7.0 – 7.5 mm, hand-matched for uniformity
  • Shape & Surface — Round, AAA+ grade, virtually flawless
  • Luster & Nacre — Mirror-bright luster, very thick nacre (0.4 mm+)
  • Clasp — Solid 18K white gold fishhook with safety catch
  • Thread — Natural silk, hand-knotted between every pearl
  • Finish — Rhodium-plated accents, hypoallergenic
  • Strand Length — 16" / 18" / 20" / 22" / 24" options
  • Total Weight — Approx. 18 g (18" strand)
How to wear +

Put on last — after perfume, hairspray, and lotion have fully absorbed. Fasten the clasp at the nape, not in front, to prevent twisting. Layer confidently with an 18" (princess length) for a silk blouse, or pair solo with a crisp collar.

Care instructions +
  • Wipe with a soft, dry cloth after each wear
  • Store flat in a soft pouch, separate from other jewelry
  • Avoid perfume, chlorine, and ultrasonic cleaners
  • Restring every 3–5 years for daily-wear pieces — complimentary for Echovelle clients

The Echovelle Craft

A Standard We Don't Compromise

From the quiet bays of Japan to the velvet-lined box on your doorstep — three decisions that define every piece we make.

No. 01 — The Pearl

Top 1% of Every Harvest

We reject 99 pearls to keep one. Mirror-bright luster, perfectly round shape, virtually flawless surface — our AAA+ standard leaves nothing to chance.

No. 02 — The Setting

Gold Clasps, Silk Knots

Solid 18K gold clasps, never plated. Every pearl is hand-knotted on silk with the traditional Japanese method — a broken strand never means lost pearls.

No. 03 — The Source

Traceable to Every Farm

Every pearl is traceable to a certified farm. A portion of every purchase funds clean-ocean initiatives — because taking from the sea means giving back to it.
