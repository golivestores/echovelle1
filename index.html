Your Pearls, Reveal Your Glow

Golden South Sea Studs

$920.00

Harvested from the pristine waters of Australia, Indonesia, and the Philippines, South Sea pearls are the largest and rarest of all cultured pearls. Measuring 9–20mm, they come in luminous white and luxurious golden hues with a soft, satiny luster.

Each South Sea pearl takes 2–4 years to form, resulting in exceptionally thick nacre and an incomparable warmth. Golden South Sea pearls are among the most coveted gems on earth — a true treasure of the deep.

Freshwater Pearl Bracelet

$165.00

Born in the rivers and lakes of China, Freshwater pearls offer remarkable versatility. Available in sizes from 4–15mm, they come in a stunning palette of white, pink, peach, and lavender, with shapes ranging from perfectly round to organic baroque.

Freshwater pearls are composed of solid nacre, giving them exceptional durability and a beautiful luminous glow. Their accessible price point and diverse styles make them the perfect entry into the world of fine pearl jewelry.

Akoya Pearl Strand Necklace

$385.00

Originating from the cool waters of Japan and China, Akoya pearls are the quintessential classic. Renowned for their perfectly round shape and mirror-like luster, they range from 6–9mm with a luminous white body and delicate rosé or silver overtones.

Akoya pearls set the standard for fine pearl jewelry. Their exceptional nacre quality creates a deep, reflective glow that has captivated collectors for centuries — the ultimate symbol of timeless elegance.

Tahitian Noir Drop Pendant

$680.00

Cultivated in the warm lagoons of French Polynesia, Tahitian pearls are nature's most dramatic gem. Ranging from 8–16mm, their dark body colors span charcoal, forest green, aubergine, and the coveted peacock — a mesmerizing play of green and purple overtones.

No two Tahitian pearls are alike. Their bold, mysterious beauty makes them a statement of individuality. The thick nacre layers create an extraordinary depth of color that shifts and shimmers with every movement.

Lumière High-Luster Ring

$445.00

Luster is the soul of a pearl — it's what separates an ordinary gem from an extraordinary one. Defined by how light interacts with the nacre layers, high luster creates a sharp, almost mirror-like reflection on the pearl's surface.

At Echovelle, we select only pearls with "very high" to "excellent" luster grades. This means you can see a clear reflection in the surface — a luminous glow that seems to radiate from within, giving each pearl its captivating inner light.

Nacre Drop Earrings

$320.00

Nacre — also known as mother-of-pearl — is the organic substance that gives a pearl its beauty. Layer upon microscopic layer of aragonite crystals are deposited by the oyster, creating the iridescent coating that makes each pearl glow.

Thicker nacre means deeper luster, richer overtones, and greater durability. Our pearls are selected for nacre thickness of 0.4mm or above, ensuring each piece will maintain its radiant beauty for generations to come.
